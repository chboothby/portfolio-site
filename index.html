A bit about me...

I'm an innate problem solver and logical thinker with a passion for developing software. When I'm not building on my coding skills, you'll find me fell-running, cycling, baking sourdough or dreaming of the 8 months I spent volunteering with alaskan huskies in Lapland.

I took a break from being a Physiotherapist to pursue my dream of working with sled dogs in Northern Sweden. During my time here that I made the decision to leave my old profession behind and make the leap to software development. My experience in healthcare has expanded my ability to think critically, communicate effectively with both other professionals and non-technical clients and recognise the importance of lifelong learning.

Since day one of my coding journey, I have relished every minute. Reflecting week on week on how much I have learnt and the projects I have built gives me immense pride. I am incredibly excited to see where the rest of this journey will take me.
