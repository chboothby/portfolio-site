about

I'm an innate problem solver and logical thinker with a passion for developing software. When I'm not building on my coding skills, you'll find me fell-running, cycling, baking sourdough or dreaming of the 8 months I spent volunteering with alaskan huskies in Lapland.

I took a break from Physiotherapy to pursue a dream of working with sled dogs in Northern Sweden. During my time here I decided to leave my old profession behind and make the leap to software development. With no prior computer science experience, I saw this as an exciting opportunity to challenge myself and open up a whole new avenue of rewarding career paths.

I have loved every minute of my fast-paced journey into software development. Reflecting each week on how much I have learned and the projects I have built has illustrated my capacity to learn new technologies quickly. I am inspired to see where the rest of this journey will take me.
